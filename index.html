About the game:

Minecraft is a video game in which allows players to explore in a three-dimensional worlds. The game's two main modes are Survival and Creative.

Creative mode

Creative mode is a game mode in Minecraft that removes health, ability to take damage, and hunger. You also have infinite building blocks and resources. This gamemode is typically for messing around, or for building

Survival mode

Survival mode is a gamemode that adds hunger, health, and the ability to take damage. In this mode, you spawn with nothing and will have to goather resources the long and hard way. You can die from damage sources like mobs, lava, fall damage, or others.
